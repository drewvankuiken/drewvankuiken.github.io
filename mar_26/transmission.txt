# Slide(30):
Header: Period 1: Entry
Break:1
Text:
Consider a firm choosing to enter the grid in location $r$:
- Let $x_{r\tau t}$ be state variables: 
$\begin{align*}x_{r\tau t} = \{q_{r\tau t}, \sum_{l_r} k^l_t, Q_{\tau t}\}\end{align*}$
- Entry incurs fixed costs $\nu_{r\tau t} + \varepsilon_{r\tau t}$. 
- Let 
$\begin{align*}v^{enter}_{r\tau t} (x_{r\tau t}) = \sum_{z=0} \delta^z \mathbb{E}_x[\pi_{r\tau t+z}|x_{r\tau t}] - \nu_{r\tau t}\end{align*}$.
- The value of entering is $v^{enter}_{r\tau t} + \varepsilon_{r\tau t}$.
IS EXPECTATION OVER X CORRECT?

# Slide(31):
Header: Choosing a region
Text: Regions vary in expected profits and fixed costs
Entrants choose 
$r \in \arg \max \rho \cdot \mathbb{E}_{\varepsilon}[v^{enter}_{r\tau t}]$
Letting $\varepsilon_{r\tau t}$ be distributed T1EV yields logsum probability of $r$:
$
\begin{align}
p(r;\tau, x_t) = \frac{exp(v)}{1 + \sum_{r^\prime \in R} exp(v_{r^\prime})}
\end{align}$
DO I NEED RHO ANYMORE?

# Slide(31):
Header: equilibrium
Text:
- Entrants track own region state vars $q_{r\tau t}, k^l_{rt}$. Assume $k$ evolves according to own region quantity and aggregate quantities only
- Entrants assume aggregate quantities evolve according to AR-1 process
- Entry forms Moment-Based Markov Equilibrium (MME) (**Ifrach and Weintraub, 2017**)

# Slide(32):
Header: Consistency
Text: Dynamic competitive equilibrium:
- Expectations about evolution of $k$ and $Q$ are correct
- Entry probabilities given by logsum prob.
- Upgrades given by upgrade policy function
- Dispatch satisfies least-cost optimization conditional on grid topology

# Slide(32):
Header: Condensing the state space
Text:
- MME helps with curse of dimensionality
- Still ~80 counties with substantial generation, 300 transmission corridors
- Proposal: using most frequently congestion lines, condense generation into ~40 regions, connected by X transmission lines, let grid evolve for each region
- Problem parallelizable

# Slide(23):
Header: Recovering Capacities
Ref:topology_details
Text:
- Initial $\bar{k}^l$: voltage
- Capacity mapping:
$k^l_t(\theta) = k^l_0 + \sum_{p=1}^P A_{lp} 1\{t\geq t_p\}\delta_{g(p)}$
- Parameters:
$\theta = (\{k^l_0\}_{l=1}^L,\{\delta_{g}\}_{g=1}^G) \, \Theta = \{k^l_0\geq 0 \forall l, \delta_{g}\geq 0 \forall g \}$

# Slide(24):
Header: Recovering Capacities
Ref:recovering_capacities
Text: Objective:
$\begin{align*}
\hat{\theta} = \arg\min_{\theta \in \Theta} 
    (m - \hat{m}(\theta))' W & (m - \hat{m}(\theta)) + \\
    & \lambda_0 \sum_{l=1}^L ((k^l_0 - \bar{k}^l)/\bar{k}^l)^2 + \lambda_1 \sum_g \delta_g^2
\end{align*}$

# Slide(33):
Ref:tech_moments
Text: **Moments**
Let $\mu^{obs}_{lt}$ denote observed shadow prices for $l$. 
Let $s^{obs}_{lt} = |\lambda^{obs}_{r(l)t} - \lambda^{obs}_{r^\prime(l) t}|$ with corresponding model moments, $r$ and $r^\prime$ connected counties. Let $\mathcal{T}_l = \{t :  \mu^{obs}_{lt} \text{ in top 20% >0 for corridor } l\}$
1. $m^{1,obs}_l = \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}_l} 1\{\mu_{lt} > 0\}$, for observed and model data
2. $m^{2,obs}_l = \frac{\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}_l} \mu_{lt}1\{\mu_{lt}>0\}}{\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}_l} 1\{\mu_{lt}>0\}}$
3. $m^{3,obs}_l = \frac{1}{|\mathcal{T}_l|} \sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}_l} s_{lt}$, for observed and model data
4. $m^{4,obs}_g = \frac{1}{|P_g|}\sum_{p:g(p)=g}[\frac{1}{|\mathcal{T}^{\text{post}}_p|}\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}^{\text{post}}_p} \mu_{l(p)t} - \frac{1}{|\mathcal{T}^{\text{pre}}_p|}\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}^{\text{pre}}_p} \mu_{l(p)t}]$, for observed and model data Notes: +++topology_gen[Back]+++
